Research

I'm interested in privacy-preserving machine learning in the form of differentially private federated learning, and the security concerns that manifest as we move training to the edge, in particular data poisoning, backdoor attacks and adversarial examples. The title of my thesis is "Challenges in Augmenting Large Language Models with Private Data".
